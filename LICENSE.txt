DATA STORAGE
All flight data is stored locally on your computer in:
- SQLite database (data/flights.db)
- Uploaded files (data/uploads/)
- Configuration (data/config/)

Your data is NEVER transmitted to external servers. This is a fully offline application.

DATA PERSISTENCE & BACKUPS
- Flight data persists indefinitely in the SQLite database
- Database is stored in your Documents folder: Documents\Skylogr\data\
- Use the "Backup Flight Data" feature regularly to protect your data
- Backups are stored in: Documents\Skylogr\data\backups\
